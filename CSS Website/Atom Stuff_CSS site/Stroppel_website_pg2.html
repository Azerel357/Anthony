The Story and Artwork

This is a back story that I came up for my currecnt character in D&D.

Story:

He doesn't remember much about his past but Alahar knew for certain that he once was human. Kidnapped by an
ancient cult, the human was sacrificed by beiing thrown thtough a portal. Once through, the human realized that he
was now in the lower planes of exictance. All of a sudden, the space in front of him started to shift, materializing into
something else. What occupiped the space in front of him was the demon Zangor, a demon of excitment. Zangor had
become bored with the world, and wanted something new. Zangor foreced the human to make a pact and then sent him
back to the mortal plane. The human had now been reborn as a Teifling (look in the D&D Handbook on the homepage to
figure out that that means). Alahar the Wicked had been created, created for the single purpose to entertain the demon and
make the world a little more...interesting.
